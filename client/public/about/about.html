With such concerning numbers, our team wanted to create an application that can allow students to budget effectively in hopes of reducing their debt.

This app was developed as a project for StemWarrior Hacks in January 2022 by Nivetha, Iqra and Tanveer. We wanted to create an application with everyday usage, and building an expense tracking website was easily one of our choices. As students we need to manage our expenses carefully and this app does just that by letting us calculate our expenses and browse previous transactions. It also provides advice to university students on budgeting, which can be viewed in the Advice option in the navigation bar. We hope that a lot of people benefit from our application that we designed to be user-friendly.
